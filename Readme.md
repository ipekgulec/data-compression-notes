# WaterWise

WaterWise is a simple SwiftUI app that helps users track their daily water intake and build healthy hydration habits.

## Inspiration
I was inspired by Nadin Tamer’s story and how she started with one simple idea and one screen.  
It showed me that even small projects can turn into meaningful experiences when they are personal and clear.

## Idea
WaterWise focuses on daily hydration tracking in a minimal and user-friendly way.  
The goal is to help users become more mindful of their water intake without overwhelming them.

## Features
- Daily water progress indicator  
- Quick add button (+250 ml)  
- Reminder option  
- Weekly statistics overview  

## Design
The interface was designed in Figma following SwiftUI and Apple Human Interface Guidelines.

## Future Improvements
- Apple Health integration  
- Custom daily goals
